I Don't Write Grants.
I Architect Funding Futures.

Hi, I'm Clint Grant. I've sat in your chair—staring at a budget deficit and a stack of rejected applications.

Now, I sit on the other side of the table, having secured over $31 Million for organizations just like yours.

From Burned-Out Director to $31M Funding Architect

I spent the first decade of my career as the Program Director for a mid-sized NGO in East Africa. I know the 3 AM anxiety of a federal NOFO drop. I know the frustration of having a world-changing program but lacking the "buzzwords" to make a foundation officer care.

I made the switch to full-time grant writing because I realized the biggest bottleneck to impact wasn't passion—it was prose and process.

Today, I leverage my insider knowledge of how grant reviewers actually score applications (spoiler: they skim first, read details later) to build proposals that are visually scannable and substantively bulletproof.
