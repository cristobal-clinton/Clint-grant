Clarity Breeds Confidence

You will never wonder "What happens next?" Here is the exact roadmap I follow from introduction to deposit.

01

The Strategy Intensive

Duration: 60-90 Minutes

This is not a sales call disguised as a consultation. This is a working session. We screen-share. We look at your website and any past grants. I ask hard questions about your logic model. You leave this call with a clear, honest assessment of your funding potential and a proposal for moving forward.

Client Action: Show up ready to talk about your budget and your biggest challenge.

02

Due Diligence & Material Gathering

Duration: 3-5 Business Days

I provide a customized checklist (Audited Financials, 990s, Board List, Program Data). You upload documents to my secure portal. I review them. If there are gaps, I flag them immediately. I will not start writing until I have what I need to win.

Client Action: Upload documents to my secure portal.

03

The Writing Sprint

Duration: 10-14 Business Days

I block my calendar. I write the first draft with the funder's review criteria open in a second window. I do not just fill in blanks; I engineer the response to score maximum points. You receive a draft that is 90% complete.

Client Action: Minimal. I may send 1-2 clarifying questions.

04

The Red Team Review

Duration: 2-3 Business Days

I ask you to read the draft like a grant reviewer: Is the case clear? Is the budget believable? We polish and finalize together. You provide one consolidated set of revisions.

Client Action: Review draft and provide ONE consolidated set of revisions.

05

Submission & Confirmation

Duration: 1 Day

I triple-check the file naming conventions, the attachments, and the certifications. We submit 48 hours before the deadline. No last-minute server crash panics.

Client Action: Hit "Submit" (or I do it for you).

06

Stewardship & Follow-Up

Duration: Ongoing

The work doesn't end at submit. I advise on thank-you letters, site visit prep, and how to handle the "decline but please apply again" letter (which is actually an invitation).

Client Action: Forward any funder correspondence to me.
