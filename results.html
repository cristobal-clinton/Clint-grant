Case Study 01

Coastal Cleanup Initiative

Environmental Nonprofit

Challenge

Operating on $50k annual budget from local donors. Needed $500k to scale water testing program but had no federal track record.

Solution

EPA Environmental Justice Grant. Repositioned their data as "Citizen Science for Public Health." Created MOU with local university for credibility.

Outcome

$487,000 Awarded

First-time federal applicant. Fully funded new lab equipment and two full-time staff.

Case Study 02

BioFiber Solutions

CleanTech Startup

Challenge

Post-seed round. Needed non-dilutive capital for pilot manufacturing line. Founders had no experience with government grants.

Solution

NSF SBIR Phase I. Developed 15-page Commercialization Plan translating complex material science into market ROI and carbon reduction metrics.

Outcome

$275,000 Phase I + $1.6M Phase II

Non-dilutive funding secured. Full-scale manufacturing underway.

Case Study 03

Harmony House

Domestic Violence Shelter

Challenge

Facility required urgent HVAC and security upgrades totaling $350k. Board writing in-house and struggling with capital campaign narrative.

Solution

HUD Community Development Block Grant (CDBG). Shifted narrative from "We need a new roof" to "Safe Shelter = Public Safety & Family Stability."

Outcome

$342,000 Awarded

Project completed on time and under budget. Capacity to serve 40% more families.
