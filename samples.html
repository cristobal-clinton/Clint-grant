Feeding America

Walmart Foundation

Type: Mobile Pantry Expansion

Industry: Hunger Relief / Food Systems

Request: $120,000

 AWARDED

"Feeding America seeks $120,000 from the Walmart Foundation to expand community-based food distribution services targeting food-insecure households..."
