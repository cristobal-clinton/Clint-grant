Tailored Strategies for Diverse Missions

The funding landscape changes dramatically depending on your tax status, sector, and geography. My approach adapts accordingly.

🏛️

Nonprofits (501c3)

Build Capacity, Not Just Projects.

I know you're tired of the "starvation cycle." I specialize in writing grants that include real overhead costs. Whether you're a $100k shop or a $5M institution, I write to scale your impact—not just your to-do list. Operating support, capital campaigns, and program expansion are all in my wheelhouse.

🏢

Small Businesses

Non-Dilutive Capital for Growth.

You don't need to give away equity to fund R&D or expansion. I help for-profit businesses navigate SBIR, STTR, state economic development grants, and workforce training reimbursements. I speak both "Investor" and "Government" fluently.

🚀

Startups (Tech & Science)

From Lab to Market.

Your technology is brilliant. Your grant writing probably isn't—yet. I help deep tech, biotech, and clean tech startups translate complex IP into compelling Commercialization Plans that the NSF, NIH, and DOE demand. Phase I, Phase II, and beyond.

🌍

NGOs / INGOs

Bridging the Global Funding Gap.

Navigating USAID subcontracts, UN agency funding, and large US foundations requires specific compliance knowledge. I work with international partners to ensure proposals meet US fiscal sponsorship requirements and donor expectations for M&E.

⚖️

Social Enterprises

The Hybrid Advantage.

You have a for-profit arm and a nonprofit mission. This is a superpower for grant writing. I help you articulate your blended value proposition and access funding sources that value both community impact and financial sustainability.

🕊️

Faith-Based Organizations

Social Services, Not Proselytizing.

I help faith-based organizations navigate the delicate line between mission and federal funding restrictions. The key is demonstrating that services are open to all, regardless of religion, and that no government funds support inherently religious activities.

Where do you fit?

Let's discuss how my experience in your sector can unlock new funding opportunities.
